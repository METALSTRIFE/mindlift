About mindlift

We’re a youth-driven startup on a mission to fight depression and social disconnection. Through interactive spaces
and meaningful experiences, we help people connect, express themselves, and feel better — mentally and emotionally.
Founded by Nouh, Belal, Lara, Mohamed and Mohamed, we believe no one should face it alone. and our slogan you will never walk alone
